Some Solutions

Scholars are calling for the reduction of plastic production, especially in the packaging sector, ie the packaging of the products we buy.

Some have already passed from theory to practice. Last year he made a lot of talk about Boyan Slat, a 21-year-old Dutchman. With its foundation Ocean Cleanup Foundation has devised a mechanism that allows to exploit the currents and waves of the sea to collect plastic waste thanks to a huge floating barrier.
